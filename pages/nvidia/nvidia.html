Team Green?

Or Team Red?

Nvidia is a leading manufacturer of GPUs. They hold the majority of the market share. But does popular always mean the best? They offer great performance, but with recent trends even the most loyal fans of Nvidia are giving up on them. The main reason - Pricing.

AMD is the second most popular GPU manufacturer. This company has existed for a long time and their product gradually got better. They have a bit better reputation currently compared to competitors for offering well performing cards for a better price.

So, which Team? IMO, it's down to personal preference. If you want to have the best performance and you have infinite money, Nvidia is the better choice. But if you aren't spending your money carelessly AMD GPUs are the perfect solution for you. It should also be mentioned that AMD gpus are more power efficient. This means that your laptop's battery with AMD GPU will last longer.
